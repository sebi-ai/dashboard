Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 26, 2026

1. Introduction

At Stardance, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website.

2. Data We Collect

  • When you connect your Google account, we collect your Google access token and refresh token, which are stored locally in your browser's localStorage.
  • We may collect your IP address for security purposes.
  • We do not collect or store any personal data on our servers.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • The data is used solely to provide you with the features of our website, such as displaying your Google Calendar events and Gmail messages.
  • We do not share your data with third parties except as required by law or with Google for OAuth authentication.

4. Data Storage

  • All data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage and is not transmitted to our servers.
  • You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

5. Your Rights

  • You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data by clearing your browser's localStorage.
  • You can revoke access to your Google account at any time.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@stardance.com.
